about us

CAPSTONE HYDROCONSULTING KENYA LTD.

Dynamic and progressive Hydrogeological and Environmental Consultancy Firm that offers sustainable development solutions to both private and public sector clients. Based in Kitale, Capstone Hydroconsulting Kenya Limited has been involved in projects in most parts of the Country. Established in 2017, Capstone HydroConsulting Kenya Limited has carried out extensive works of large, medium and small scale, with varying degrees of complexity and environmental concern, and are continuously involved in turn key projects. Capstone Hydroconsulting Kenya Limited Commits itself to good governance, ethical behavior and seeks to be trustworthy, respectful and socially responsible so as to promote and pursue excellence in the course of providing consultancy, research and training services in all water and environmental aspects.
